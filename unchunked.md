link_index(node_index, degree)
= node_index * (1 << (degree + 1)) + (1 << degree) - 1
= node_index << (degree + 1) + (1 << degree) - 1
= (node_index << 1) << degree + (1 << degree) - 1
= (node_index << 1 + 1) << degree - 1
